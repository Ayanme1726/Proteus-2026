## 💡 Tips for Success

Keep your schematic organized. Use labels for power and ground lines to prevent clutter. Run regular simulations as you add sections to your design. This makes it easier to find bugs. If the simulation fails, check your power connections first. Most errors occur because of missing connections between components.
